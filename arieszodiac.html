Aries Zodaic Constellation

The First Sign of the Zodiac.

The Origin

Historically, Aries has represented a ram since late Babylonian times, though earlier it was known as "The Agrarian Worker" or "The Hired Man" in Babylonian astronomy. The shift in identification from a farmhand to a ram likely occurred due to its association with the god Dumuzi the Shepherd.

Dates

Governs the period from March 20 to April 19. So anyone that was born between those dates is a Aries.

Location

It is located in the Northern celestial hemisphere, situated between Pisces to the west and Taurus to the east, and lies along the ecliptic, the apparent path of the Sun across the sky. The best observed from September through February, with the optimal viewing time around 9 PM in early December.

Element

In astrology, Aries is a fire sign and a cardinal sign, ruled by the planet Mars, which governs action, war, and passion.

Aries Traits in Sun, Moon, and Ascendant Signs

  • Typical Traits

    In astrology, Aries is a fire sign and a cardinal sign, ruled by the planet Mars, which governs action, war, and passion. They are direct and honest in their communication, often expressing their feelings and opinions without hesitation.

  • As Sun Sign

    An Aries Sun Sign signifies a confident, assertive, and independent individual who is driven by a strong desire for action, adventure, and leadership.

  • As Moon Sign

    An Aries Moon Sign indicates a person whose emotional self closely aligns with their Sun sign, resulting in a passionate, impulsive, and highly expressive nature.

  • As Ascendant (Raising) Sign

    An Aries Rising person is known as a go-getter, projecting confidence, assertiveness, and independence.
