What are they?

Your Sun, Moon, and Ascendant (Rising) signs are the three core components of your astrological chart, each representing a different facet of your personality. Your Sun sign defines your core identity, ego, and the essence of who you are, representing your drive and overall attitude.

Sun, Moon, and Ascendant Signs

  • As Sun Sign

    It is determined by your birth date and is the sign most commonly associated with your zodiac sign. Represents your core identity, ego, and motivations. It is the key factor in assessing your overall personality and spirit.

  • As Moon Sign

    Unlike the Sun sign, the Moon sign changes approximately every 2.5 days, so determining it accurately requires your exact time of birth. Reflects your emotional nature, subconscious mind, inner world, and instinctive reactions. It reveals your emotional needs and how you seek comfort.

  • As Ascendant (Raising) Sign

    The Rising sign changes roughly every two hours, making an exact birth time crucial for an accurate calculation. Represents your outward personality, first impressions, and the way you carry yourself in daily life.
