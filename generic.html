The Origin

The term "zodiac" originates from the Greek word for "circle of animals," reflecting that many of these constellations represent animals or mythical creatures

The 12 traditional Zodiac Constellations

Aries, Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpius, Sagittarius, Capricornus, Aquarius, and Pisces.
